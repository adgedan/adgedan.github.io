THE QUALITY OF LIGHT MATTERS

Soraa pioneered full spectrum color rendering LED light, the heart of which is the proprietary LED technology manufactured in California, a technological breakthrough that has been enhanced by focusing on every aspect of producing the most natural light possible.

VIVID COLOUR

Utilizing every color in the rainbow, especially deep red emission, Soraa’s VP₃ VIVID COLOR™ renders warm tones beautifully and accurately.

NATURAL WHITES

Soraa’s Violet-Emission 3-Phosphor (VP₃) LED technology allows for perfect rendering of colors and whiteness.

FLICKER FREE

Soraa’s digital driver completely addresses the adverse physiological effects associated with invisible flicker.

BEAM, SHADDOW, UNIFORMITY

Neither halogen nor other LED competitors can create point sources small enough to achieve these narrow beam angles and high center beam intensity.
