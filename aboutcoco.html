 ABOUT COCO

Based in Braintree, Essex within our historic 30,000 sq ft Victorian power station we have been successfully servicing the lighting industry for well over 35 years.

Our emergency lighting and control solutions utilise the state of the art technology to provide high performance, high reliability and cost effective lighting systems. We continually invest in our team of staff and employ the latest HAL testing and EMC equipment enabling us to define the marking, testing and responsibility requirements for re-engineering luminaires into fully compliant emergency lighting to comply with the current ICEL 1004: 2014 standards.

These investments, along with our internal systems, practices and quality control are designed to define the marking, testing and responsibility requirements for re-engineering luminaires into emergency lighting complying, with:

EN 60598-1 - EN 60598-2-22 - EN 61347-1
EN 61347-2-3 Annex J - EN 61347-2-7 - EN 61347-2-13
EN 55015 - EN 61547 - EN 61000-3-2
