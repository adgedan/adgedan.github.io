Let me tell you a story.... I am starting the group because all of the detecting groups around where I live (Braintree & South East Essex) are closed to new members.  I was told by one, and I quote (we are not taking new members until next September) What year was that ???????????????. They then hung up the phone. Either they are full or they dont want to know or its a closed knit community...probably the latter.

Any way enough about the others. Let me tell you about Braintree Metal Detecting Club. We are in the middle of getting the club put in all metal detecting Magazines.

We will be Affiliated to the National Council for Metal Detecting.  And if you are not a member you can join through the Club. I am a member myself and it gives you Insurance Cover up to £250,000 and legal Assistance.

I have arranged for special discount for buying Detectors and detector bits :ie: Digers, Pouches. Hats. etc Through Regton Metal Detectors.

We will be in the Treasure Hunting Magazine Soon that is published by Green Light Publishing of Witham.. Just up the road from me. The Club will be holding a OPEN rally every year at a price on at least 100 to 200 acres of lan after the first harvest.
Members pay a reduced fee and it is open to the public so they can se what we find. BRING THE KIDS and Colchester Museum Can Record it all. All proceeds go towards BMDC funds to purchase detecting rights on farmers land for the club.

Brian Day

Club Founder

When and where do we meet?

We meet at the Cross Keys pub in White Notley, Essex, CM8 1RQ at 7pm on the first Wednesday of every month.
