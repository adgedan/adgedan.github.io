EMERGENCY LIGHTING

We are registered to ICEL standard that guarantees all our lighting conversions are carried out to comply with strict ICEL requirements. Ensuring your lighting is effective in a building emergency, especially when the normal mains supply fails, when fast panic-free evacuation is absolutely essential.

Our team of highly experienced trained conversion specialists use only quality components within our ESD protected facility . All inspections and tests on conversions are carried out on each individual unit for peace of mind.
