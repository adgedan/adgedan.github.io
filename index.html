ABOUT

Formed in 2004, Knight Controls work in a multitude of market sectors, from Leisure centres, schools, colleges, large department stores and retails outlets including hospitals and restaurants. We specialise in offering installation, engineering, servicing and the maintenance of analogue or digital systems including graphic and front end PC’s and monitoring systems.

These can range from a simple thermostat control to a wide scale Building Management System covering 30+ office floors. We also have the capability for electrical installations and the design, manufacture and modification of new an existing BMS control panels.

BMS & HVAC PANEL UPGRADES

Unlike many BMS installation companies we manage the design, manufacture and installation of all our control panels in-house and provide a validation certificate upon completion.

All panels undergo stringent testing and sign off prior to installation on our customers premises ensuring complete confidence that project timescales and budgets are kept within reach with not interuption to your BMS upgrade at any time.

BUILDING MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS

If you have an old building management system (BMS) Knight Controls can help you create and deliver a cost effective upgrade path without the need for a substantial up-front investment. Upgrading to a newer system, or reviewing your strategy, could result in significant energy savings.

In many cases, modern equipment can be directly integrated with older products via programmable gateways, bespoke engineered solutions or even via open protocols. Knight Controls can ensure you benefit from the most appropriate solution for your own specific circumstances.

Our fully trained team take on a variety of systems daily and we pride ourselves on our ability to retrofit modern BMS into existing panels (in place of older or obsolete BMS systems), or to simply replace the system completely including panels. Not a problem.
