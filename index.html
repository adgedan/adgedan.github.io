EMERGENCY LIGHTING

Installing emergency lighting systems enables a building to meet fire safety legislation in a way that is visually acceptable and meets the user’s needs for ease of operation and maintenance. It is important to establish all the relevant legal requirements for emergency lighting and fire alarm systems before commencing a new design or conversion. 

Experience

Coco Lighting have been carrying out emergency lighting conversions & design for over 30 years. All our re-engineering & emergency luminaires comply to the strict ICEL 1004: 2014 criteria.

Flexibility

Reputation

Customer service and our reputation within the industry is paramount for Coco lighting. Over the years we have adopted systems and processes in place to ensure the quality of our service and products is second to none.

LIGHTING CONTROLS

Lighting is responsible for about 35% of the electricity usage in a typical building?
Much of it is wasted due to poor lighting design, inefficient lighting equipment, and poor controls. Recognising this, Coco Lighting can offer many solutions to enable full control of your lighting whether residential, commercial, institutional, and industrial facilitieswith versatile and automated controls.

Energy Saving

Coco Lighting control solutions are flexible and designed to reduce the amount of energy used for lighting, while ensuring ergonomic comfort. From small devices like timers and occupancy sensors, to sophisticated customised solutions based on DALIcontrol, C-Bus.

Benefits

Helping reduce or eliminates reliance on occupants or employees to turn lights off automatically can result in cost savings, extended lamp life, environmental benefits along with time spent on lowering overall maintenance costs within your building are just a few benefits installing controls.

Control

Coco Lighting can give you full control and visibility of your entire lighting system from just a simple mobile hand held device on site or cloud based control anywhere in the world. From wireless controls to low voltage solutions we can ensure you have full control.

LIGHTING CONVERSIONS

We are a full lighting conversion service enabling our clients to reduce lead times and offering improved and alternative product solutions. We are specialists in this service with over 20 years experience in carrying out this work for electrical wholesalers, lighting importers, and LED Lighting companies.

Conversion

We convert standard, LED and Fluorescent Luminaires including emergency variants with the following options:, DALI, Self-Test, Maintained, Non-Maintained, Sustained
 and Central Battery Backup. We always advise on the best value option for each client son an individuals requirements.

Guarantee

All our converted luminaires are subject to rigorous test procedures and guaranteed for at least 12 months. There is no specific British Standard covering Emergency Lighting conversions, however all our conversion work carried out meets and exceeds the relevant test requirements.

Benefits

The initial benefits of  LED lighting is the long-term cost savings. Yes, bulb-for-bulb, LED lighting does have a higher initial cost compared to incandescent lighting. However, over the course of an LED lifespan, the real-life costs are a mere fifth of incandescent lighting.

At Coco lighting we use the latest Seaward HAL testing and EMC equipment and with our internal operations, practices and quality control systems that are designed to define the marking, testing and responsibility requirements for re-engineering
luminaires into emergency lighting fully complying with the following requirements:

• EN 60598-1 • EN 60598-2-22 • EN 61347-1 • EN 61347-2-3 Annex J • EN 61347-2-7 
• EN 61347-2 13 • EN 55015 • EN 61547 • EN 61000-3-2
